## 💡 How It Works

1. **Upload Your Guidelines** - Upload your Language Book or Brand Guidelines (TXT file)
2. **AI Extracts Criteria** - The AI reads your document and identifies your brand voice characteristics
3. **Analyze Content** - Test any content (blog posts, social media, emails) against YOUR brand standards
4. **Get Feedback** - Receive scores and specific suggestions for improvement

## ✨ Features

- **Upload Guidelines**: AI extracts brand voice criteria from your documents
- **Train on Examples**: Paste 3-5 content samples and AI learns your patterns  
- **Industry Presets**: Pre-built templates for Healthcare, Luxury, B2B, Hospitality
- **Before/After Compare**: See improvements side-by-side
- **Free to Use**: No sign-up, no download required
